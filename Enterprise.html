NETWORK INFRASTRUCTURE

Network infrastructure refers to the whole network's hardware and software resources that allow for network connectivity, communication, operation, and management. It offers the channels and services for communication between people, operations, software, services, and external networks/the internet. A company's productivity and profitability in the modern digital era depend on more than just talented, diligent workers and high-functioning technology like computers, printers, and phones. A strong and secure network infrastructure is necessary for a business to operate properly. Without a solid infrastructure in place, a business may encounter bad user experiences that harm staff or consumers, as well as security problems that can reduce employee productivity, drive up expenses, or even hurt your brand. It's critical to comprehend network infrastructure and why it's important for your company.
