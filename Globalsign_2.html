Who are GlobalSign

GlobalSign, which was founded over 25 years ago, is a trusted identity and security solutions provider that enables enterprises all over the world to conduct secure online communications, maintain verified digital identities, and automate authentication and encryption. Its identity and security services are offered through a WebTrustTM audited strong infrastructure that includes worldwide datacenters, redundancy, high availability, world-class network instrumentation, and vast scalability. It installs public and private PKI hierarchies via cloud services, with direct integration into local/cloud directories, extended customer/partner databases, and enterprise applications and networks. GlobalSign's public root key, a distinguishing feature, is by default integrated in all major Internet programs and browsers. As a result, GlobalSign certificates are universally recognized and are not restricted to any application, geographic region, or business sector. Over 5 million digital certificates have been issued, and over 25 million certificates globally rely on the public confidence supplied by the GlobalSign root. GlobalSign has locations in the Americas, Europe, Asia, and Africa. Its major offices, with support agencies all around the world, are in the United States, in New Hampshire, and in the United Kingdom, in Kent.
